## History

KSC Wiedikon was founded in 1982 in Zürich-Wiedikon as a pure volleyball club. A group of passionate athletes from the neighborhood came together to pursue their love of the sport. What started with a single men's team in the lowest league quickly grew into a staple of Zurich's recreational sports scene. Already in the first few years, the club was able to steadily build new teams thanks to committed members and strong youth development.

In the early 1990s, KSC Wiedikon expanded its offering to include basketball — a step that would shape the club for years to come. The combination of two indoor sports under one roof proved unique in Zurich and attracted more and more members over the years. Today, KSC Wiedikon counts over 300 active members across more than 20 teams, from regional leagues to recreational sports, making it one of the largest multi-sport clubs in District 3. The close connection to the Wiedikon neighborhood, the family atmosphere, and the passion for sport continue to form the foundation of the club.
